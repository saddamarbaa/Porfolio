my Story

Created by Saddam Arbaa on : 10/20/2020


Indonesia 2019
I am Sundaes refugee currently lives in Indonesia, Asurvivor of Genocide that’s still taking place in Darfur.
I lived a very difficult life in my childhood because I didn't have a school near my village, therefore, I had to wake up early morning every day and go three hours by a donkey to another city for school to attend my classes and three hours to came back home. It was very scary to go alone way in the early morning because I was the only child in the village who would go to school. My parents were so happy that one day I will become a teacher and open school in the village so that other children will not suffer like I did.
